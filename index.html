Akan Naming System

The web service that gives you an African name

Welcome to Akan naming Web service


The Akan naming System is a web application that is based on a Ghananian naming culture. The Ghananian Akan naming works as follows;

Children are given their first names as a day name which corresponds to the day of the week they were born.


How it Works

The application takes your birthday and gender input and tells the day of the week you were born, and then gives you an Akan name corresponding to your day of birth.

The system works in three easy steps:

  1. Enter your birthday on the form below
  2. Select your gender
  3. click on submit button to view the results
